## Caveats (AGENTS.md §1 empirical honesty)

- **2WikiMultiHopQA** is the only dataset with native triple-level gold paths (`evidences`), hence the lead role (100% gold).
- **MuSiQue** gold% (66%) reflects that only ~2/3 of decomposition hops use the structured `entity >> relation` template; the rest are natural-language sub-questions. The full raw decomposition is kept in `meta['decomposition']` for P2 KG construction.
- **WebQSP / CWQ** have no loader-side gold paths or (CWQ) anchors: these are encoded in Freebase/SPARQL and materialized in P2 once the Freebase endpoint is available (§3.3, [HUMAN-REQUIRED]).
- **MetaQA / HotpotQA** have anchors but no triple gold paths; MetaQA paths come from its self-contained KB, HotpotQA from text-constructed KGs (P2).
- **WebQSP/CWQ dataset-quality caveat** (AGENTS.md §1, arXiv:2505.23495): cleaned-subset filtering is applied downstream, not at load time.
