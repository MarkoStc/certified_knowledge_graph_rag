# Stage-1 de-risk gate (P7) — certified k vs empirical robustness

Generated by `scripts/make_stage1_report.py`. Tests H1: does the
deletion certificate `k` predict robustness to the RAG-Safety insertion
attack? For MetaQA 2-hop queries stratified by `k`, the reasoner
(Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct) answers from clean supporting evidence and from
evidence + a forged competing chain to a type-consistent wrong answer.
**Flip rate** = fraction of *clean-correct* queries whose answer flips to
the forged wrong answer under attack. Prediction: flip rate falls as `k`
rises (more independent real support resists a forged path).
